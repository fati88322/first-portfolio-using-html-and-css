GRAPHIC DESIGN

A graphic designer is a creative professional who uses visual elements to communicate ideas and messages. They design a wide range of materials, including logos, websites, advertisements, brochures, magazines, and product packaging. Graphic designers combine art and technology to create visually appealing and effective designs that meet their clients' objectives. They utilize tools like Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
